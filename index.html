Why I started this project

This is not a big company product. It began as my own way of saying sorry and goodbye to my brother.

Personal story

My name is LeiMai. In 2023, my older brother was struggling badly with money and life. I thought giving him space might help, so I slowly distanced myself.

On New Yearâ€™s Eve of 2024, he took his own life by charcoal burning. For a long time, I could not forgive myself.

Months later, I found our old childhood photos. I used AI to make them move again â€” just small motions: a blink, a breath, a tiny smile.

When I watched that short animated photo, I cried harder than ever, but something inside finally softened. It reminded me of how much love was still there, beyond all my regrets.

That moment changed me. I realized this technology should not only be for fun or filters. It can help people hold a quiet, healing moment with someone they miss deeply.

This is why I offer one free memory animation for everyone. If it touches your heart, you can choose to create more.

How it works

A simple four-step process. You first share your story and who is in the photo. I then ask you for the actual file by email and create a private 8-second memory just for you.

Step 1
Fill a short, gentle form

Tell me who is in the photo with you, what your relationship is, and what kind of feeling you hope to touch again. No files yet, only words.

Step 2
I reply and ask for your photo

After I read your message, I personally reply to your email. You can then send one group photo as an attachment in a private conversation.

Step 3
I craft a short animated memory

Using AI and manual tweaking, I bring subtle movement into the photo: breathing, blinking, a soft head tilt or smile. No jokes, no exaggeration. Each clip is around 8 seconds in 1080p.

Step 4
Receive a private preview

Within about 24 hours after you send your photo, you receive a downloadable ~8 second video by email or Telegram. If it feels right, you can ask for more memories or a longer story video.

Privacy, consent, and respect first

These are not just files. They are people you love. I handle every project with the same care I wish I had shown my brother when he was still here.

  • I never publish or share your photos or videos.
  • All raw files are deleted within 24 hours after final delivery.

Questions you might have

What kind of photo works best?

A clear group photo where faces are visible and not heavily blurred. Old printed photos are fine as long as you can see the eyes and expressions.

Can I use very old or low-quality photos?

Yes. I will do my best with what you have. Sometimes the imperfections add to the feeling. If a photo is not suitable, I will tell you honestly.

How long does it take to receive the preview?

Usually within about 24 hours after you send your photo. If I am overloaded, I will inform you before starting.

How do I pay in USDT if I want more?

After you confirm you want a paid memory, I send you a USDT wallet address (TRC20 or ERC20) and the exact amount. You keep the transaction ID as proof.

Can you add sound or voice?

For now I focus on silent or soft-music videos, not synthetic voices. In the future, I may add gentle voice options if they can be done respectfully.

What if I do not like the result?

Tell me honestly. For free previews there is no problem at all. For paid memories we can adjust within reason, or I can delete everything if you prefer.
